Mission Statement

I am a passionate educator who believes in teaching the “whole child” as they learn to become the very best versions of themselves. I believe wholeheartedly in fostering an environment in which students learn just as much about working together, solving real-life problems, celebrating differences, persevering through challenges, and helping one another, as they do about literacy and mathematics. I believe that we all achieve more when we are inspired through encouragement and positive reinforcement and work diligently to provide a classroom culture founded on these beliefs.
